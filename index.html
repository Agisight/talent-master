The problem

People do meaningful work for years, but their achievements are scattered across emails, screenshots, files, links, publications, apps, and memory.

01

Evidence gets lost

Important proof is buried across inboxes, cloud folders, chats, websites, and screenshots.

02

Resumes are too flat

Modern careers are multidimensional: engineering, research, leadership, community, media, and product impact.

03

People start too late

They reconstruct years of work only when applying for a visa, grant, founder program, school, or senior role.

What Talent Master does

A mobile-first iPad/macOS experience for low-friction capture, AI-assisted structuring, and scoped access to the right slice of your profile.

Capture

Save proof instantly

Add screenshots, links, documents, publications, app releases, recommendations, awards, and metrics.

Organize

Build talent tracks

AI connects evidence into tracks such as engineering, research, leadership, product impact, education, and recognition.

Guide

Know what to do next

The graph shows gaps, strengths, and next actions for career, education, immigration, grants, and recognition.

High-stakes use cases

  • Talent visas: EB-1A, O-1, global talent routes
  • Grants, fellowships, founder programs, accelerators
  • University applications and academic recognition
  • Senior roles, expert positioning, and public credibility

Why it is different

  • Not a resume builder
  • Not a document folder
  • A living graph of verifiable achievements
  • Scoped access: share only the relevant slice with an expert, organization, or AI system

Built from a real evidence workflow, not from a resume template.

Privacy

Talent Master is designed as a local-first productivity tool. The app can be tested without creating an account.

Local-first data handling

Achievements, events, media references, saved feed items, AI chat history, preferences, and guest-mode data are stored on the user's device. Talent Master does not operate a central backend that lets other testers or users see someone else's local workspace.

If a user chooses to connect an external AI provider or account credential, those credentials are used for the selected integration and should be managed by the user. AI-generated content is informational and may be inaccurate.

No test account is required for review or beta testing. Launch the app and choose Continue as a Guest.
